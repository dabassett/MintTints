MintTints

Welcome to MintTints!

Here to help designers pair text and background colors that are both beautiful and easy to read

MintTints can take any color and generate analogous colors that satisfy WCAG 2.0 contrast ratio requirements. This means that you no longer need to fiddle with finicky formulae to create perfect color palettes.

Powered by HSWL

Functional yet familiar

HSWL is a hybrid color space that combines the hue and saturation attributes from HSL and replaces lightness with WCAG's luminance formula. This connection allows you to specify colors by a desired contrast directly, without any of the "guess and check" methods that would otherwise be required.
